  • The game has 2 players, playing in rounds
  • In each turn, a player rolls two dices as many times as he whishes. Each result get added to his CURRENT ROUND score
  • BUT, if the player rolls a 1 on any of the dices, all his CURRENT ROUND score gets lost. After that, it's the next player's turn
  • The player can choose to 'Hold', which means that his CURRENT ROUND score gets added to his GLOBAL score
  • After that, it's the next player's turn
  • The first player to reach 100 points or the value entered, on GLOBAL score wins the game
